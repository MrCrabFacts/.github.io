KIYOMIZUDERA TEMPLE - KYOTO





The Kiyomizudera Temple is a large buddhist temple in the heart of Kyoto.






There's a few old traditions here, first being 'Stage Jumping', where people would jump off the nearly 43 foot high stage, believing that if they survived, their wish would be granted, naturally, this was banned after people died and got hurt (with a shocking 85% SURVIVAL RATE)



The second (and far safer) is drinking from the Kiyomizudera Waterfall. The Kiyomizudera waterfall is located just below the temple, and drinking from on of three streams supposedly has various effects, the 1st is Health/Longevity, the 2nd is Academic Success, and the 3rd and final is Success in love.
