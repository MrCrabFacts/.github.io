KINKAKU-JI - KYOTO


Kinkaku-Ji is a small pavillion in Kyoto, known for its Reliquary which acts as a centerpiece, which is (unsurprisingly) coated in gold leaf!


The gold leaf pavillion is one of the 17 Ancient Monuments of Kyoto and is a world heritage site


The gold leaf covering the top 2 floors of the reliquary is approximately 0.5 Î¼m (Mircometers) Thick.
