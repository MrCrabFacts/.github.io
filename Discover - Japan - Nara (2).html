NARA PARK - NARA


Nara Park is (who could have guessed) a small park in Nara, known for the friendly wandering deer, and the Todaiji Temple.


The deer will actually bow back if you bow at them! (this is usually due to the fact that they get food when they do)


The Todaiji Temple is known for being one of the largest in japan, however it actually used to be bigger, as it was destroyed 2 times by fires in 1180 and 1567 and had to be rebuilt
