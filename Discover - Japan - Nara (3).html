THE SHINKANSEN - NARA


The Shinkansen is a series of trains all throughout japan, allowing quick and relatively cheap travel across long distances.


The Shinkansen is known best for being the fastest train in the world, running at 285 km/h (or 177mph) earning it the nickname 'The Bullet Train'


The reason it's placed in Nara, is because we rode it from Nara, all the way into the next place we went, Tokyo!
