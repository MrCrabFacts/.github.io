DON QUIJOTE'S!!!


An extraordinarily popular chain of markets in Japan that can span from 2-8 stories tall!! And let me tell you, they carry LITERALLY EVERYTHING YOU WILL EVER NEED. (I went there looking for a duffel bag and I got quite possibly the largest duffel bag ever for a whopping $20).


Now, you may be asking "Why the hell are you including a Japanese store chain in this presentation"?


Let me tell you, walking inside of this place by myself was quite possibly one of the most confusing and overstimulating experiences you can think of (but in a strangely fun way?). Loud catchy music, aisles of brightly colored god-knows-what in it, and bright lights everywhere, combine that with a huge floor layout, multiply it by 8, and you get this store.


Also, the music is strangely catchy, and istg I could not stop humming it when I got home, best fever dream ever, 10/10 would wander blindly down aisles looking for checkout again.
