YUSHUKAN WAR MUSEUM - TOKYO


During a free day in Tokyo, my group went and visited the Yushukan War Museum, which was full of various war artifacts.


It had lots of objects, planes, bombs, swords, guns, outfits of military leaders, it even had a few documentaries playing!


Another neat part about it is the stamp books they sell for 100 Yen, and different spots of the museum gave you something to stamp the book with, so you would have a Keepsake to bring home!


Unfortunately, we had to leave 2 days later :(
